About Me

Hi there! Thank you for stopping by and exploring my work. My name is Kanda, and I have been doing photography professionally for 3 years now! However, since young, I have always been into photography. I got my first DSLR camera in my freshman year of highschool. I knew that I always wanted a nice camera at a very young age. I was drawn by the quality that were produced from the DSLR cameras, so I had to get my hands on one. I love capturing special moments for everyone. Time will pass by, but the memories will not. I am confident that I can make my clients happy, and I am also confident in delivering the best quality images. I would love to work with you and help capture a special moment in your life!
